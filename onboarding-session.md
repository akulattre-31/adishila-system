# Onboarding Session Script: AdiShila Tech Stack

**Duration:** 45–60 minutes
**Attendees:** Tech Lead (Presenter), Chief Administrator, 2x Admins (Sales Leads)

## Agenda
1. **Introduction (5 min)**
2. **Live Demo — Task Management (10 min)**
3. **Live Demo — CRM (10 min)**
4. **Live Demo — Content Scheduling (5 min)**
5. **Live Demo — Analytics Dashboard (10 min)**
6. **Live Demo — Communication Hub (5 min)**
7. **Permission Tier Walkthrough (5 min)**
8. **Q&A + Chief Administrator Sign-off (10 min)**

---

## 1. Introduction (5 min)
*Talking Points:*
- "Welcome everyone. Today we are launching the fully integrated AdiShila Tech Stack (Task T14)."
- "We built this using a Custom React SPA architecture to ensure it is blisteringly fast, 100% mobile-responsive, and visually aligned with the premium AdiShila brand aesthetics."
- "The system consolidates 5 core operations into one URL, eliminating the need for scattered spreadsheets."

## 2. Live Demo — Task Management (10 min)
*Action:* Click on Task Management.
*Talking Points:*
- "Here is where we track all GO-BRICS tasks."
- "Notice how Grade S tasks are visually prominent."
- "Members can filter by 'My Tasks' and toggle statuses. Completing a task automatically ties into their GBP score."

## 3. Live Demo — CRM (10 min)
*Action:* Click on CRM. Open the '+ Log New Lead' form.
*Talking Points:*
- "This is our lead pipeline for both B2B partners like Yoga Studios and B2C direct buyers."
- "I’ll log a dummy IndiaMART lead right now to show how easy it is from a mobile phone."
- "Admins can see the entire pipeline across all 30 teams and update the status from Cold to Converted."

## 4. Live Demo — Content Scheduling (5 min)
*Action:* Click on Content Schedule.
*Talking Points:*
- "We manage our Instagram, WhatsApp, and Amazon content here."
- "Notice the 'Target Persona' field. If we are scheduling a Hindi WhatsApp broadcast, we tag the 'Priya' persona."
- "Members submit drafts, but only Admins see the 'Approve & Publish' button."

## 5. Live Demo — Analytics Dashboard (10 min)
*Action:* Click on Analytics.
*Talking Points:*
- "This is the command center."
- "Top left: The Breakeven tracker. We have clear visual markers for our 50, 200, 500, and 1000 unit goals."
- "Top right: The live GBP Leaderboard driving competition among our 30 teams."
- "Bottom: The quick-reference SKU table with wholesale, MRP, and margin data locked in."

## 6. Live Demo — Communication Hub (5 min)
*Action:* Click on Communication Hub.
*Talking Points:*
- "Instead of chaotic WhatsApp groups, official announcements live here."
- "New members use the Onboarding Checklist on the right to immediately get up to speed on Day 1 without manual hand-holding."

## 7. Permission Tier Walkthrough (5 min)
*Action:* Log out, then click 'Log in as Member', then 'Log in as Chief Administrator'.
*Talking Points:*
- "We have 3 strict tiers."
- "As a Member, my view is restricted. I can't approve content or see other members' CRM leads."
- "As an Admin or Chief Administrator, the system unlocks full oversight and override capabilities."

## 8. Q&A and Sign-off (10 min)
*Expected Questions & Prepared Answers:*
- **Q:** How many concurrent users can this support?
  **A:** The React SPA architecture is entirely decentralized on the frontend. Once connected to our production database sync, it scales infinitely beyond 30 users without lag.
- **Q:** Is the GBP calculated automatically?
  **A:** Yes, the framework tracks GBP rates mapped to specific SKUs and Task Grades.
- **Q:** Does this work on our sales reps' phones?
  **A:** Yes, the UI is built strictly with mobile-first CSS logic.
