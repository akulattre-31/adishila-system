# AdiShila Onboarding & Training Session Notes

## Welcome to the AdiShila Platform

The AdiShila Integrated Tech Stack serves as the unified hub for our 30 field teams and 5 administrators to collaborate, track progress, and earn **Go-Brics Points (GBP)**. 

### Step 1: Initial Login
1. Navigate to the application.
2. Select your designated role (e.g., Demo Member, Sales Lead Admin, Chief Administrator).
3. If the platform lacks data upon launch, the Chief Administrator can click **"Seed Initial Data"** from the Analytics dashboard to populate the environment.

### Step 2: The Communication Hub
1. Open the **Communication Hub** from the sidebar (or bottom nav on mobile).
2. Read the latest team announcements.
3. Review your **Onboarding Checklist**. Your progress is automatically saved to the cloud. 
4. Once you have checked all 4 items, click "Complete Onboarding". A system announcement will automatically broadcast your achievement to the team.

### Step 3: Managing the CRM Pipeline
1. Head to the **CRM** module.
2. You can search for leads by name, city, or phone number.
3. Filter leads by B2B/B2C Tier, Lead Status, or Assignee.
4. Admins have access to an **Assign** dropdown in the Actions column. Assign leads to your teammates so they can take ownership.
5. Click column headers to sort the data (ascending/descending).

### Step 4: Execution & GBP Earnings
- **Task Management**: Click on a task to open the comment thread. Chat with your teammates to coordinate. Change a task status to "Completed" to instantly credit your assigned account with the corresponding GBP.
- **Content Scheduler**: View upcoming social media posts. Admins can click "Approve & Publish", which updates the post status and automatically rewards the author with GBP based on the SKU's predefined rate.

### Step 5: Real-Time Analytics
1. The **Analytics** module provides live, auto-updating SVG visualizations of our pipeline funnel and content timeline.
2. Click **Export Report** to generate a local `.csv` file snapshot of the system state for external reporting.

## Key Policies
- Do not spam the Notification Center.
- Only Admins and Chief Administrators can post global announcements or seed the database.
- Keep your CRM notes concise but detailed.
